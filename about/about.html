About

Camilla Padgitt-Coles and Jessa farkas formed Future Shuttle in 2008 in Greenpoint, Brooklyn, after graduating from Oberlin College in 2007, where the both friends were DJs at 91.5FM WOBC Oberlin College and Community Radio. Flautist Lizzie Harper and Drummer Linnea Vedder joined in for the creation and release of Etude Study, FRKWYS 10, and In To It. Camilla and Jessa have since been slowly creating a new improvised ambient album, to be released on cassette in 2019 by Lillerne Tapes.
